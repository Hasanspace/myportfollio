Featured Project

Professional Book Design & Layout

Project Scope: E-book and Print Book Interior Design

Overview and Challenge

This project involved designing the complete interior layout, typography, and chapter styling for a 300-page business management book. The main challenge was ensuring readability across multiple formats (e-book, paperback, and hardcover) while maintaining a modern and clean aesthetic.

Execution & Outcome

I utilized Adobe InDesign to create a structured grid system, chose the 'Georgia' font family for its legibility, and developed custom master pages for headers and footers. The result was a professional-grade, print-ready file that the client successfully launched on Amazon and major publishing platforms.
